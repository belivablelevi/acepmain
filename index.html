How ACEP Works

Four simple steps to start making environmental impact

1. Create Profile

Join our community of environmental champions. Set up your profile and choose your focus areas.

2. Accept Challenges

Browse curated environmental missions. From water testing to pollution reporting.

3. Take Action

Complete challenges in your community. Document your impact with photos and verification.

4. Earn & Compete

Get points for verified actions. Unlock badges, climb the leaderboard, and inspire others.
