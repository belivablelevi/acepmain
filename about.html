About ACEP

Alliance for Community Environmental Protection

Our Mission

Fighting for Future Generations

ACEP is dedicated to protecting our planet through community-driven environmental action. We focus on combating the most pressing pollution threats facing our ecosystems today: industrial and agricultural runoff, wastewater contamination, harmful substances, and eutrophication.

By gamifying environmental action, we make it engaging and rewarding for individuals to make a real difference. Every challenge completed, every pollution source reported, every water body monitored contributes to a healthier planet.
